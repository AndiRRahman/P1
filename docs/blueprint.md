# **App Name**: E-Commers V

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing (Guest): Allow guests to view products on the landing page, redirecting them to login/register upon clicking a product.
- Product Browsing (User): Enable users to browse products and view detailed product information.
- Shopping Cart: Allow users to add products to a shopping cart and view/manage its contents.
- Order Checkout: Enable users to checkout their orders.
- Order History: Allow users to view their order history in their profile.
- Product Management (Admin): Enable admin to add, edit, and delete products.
- Order Management (Admin): Enable admin to view incoming orders and update their status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#673AB7) to evoke trust and quality.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F5F5F5), near-white, to give prominence to product photography.
- Accent color: Analogous blue (#3F51B5), a little less saturated and darker than the primary, to signal interactive elements.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headings and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use minimalist line icons for navigation and product categories.
- Use a grid-based layout for product listings, with clear spacing and visual hierarchy.
- Subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback on user interactions.
